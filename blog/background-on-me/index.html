Background on Me

Jan 17th, 2015

My name is Mark Fussell, and I have an unusually large set of honed skills. These I acquired by surviving Caltech, progressing through a total of ten+ startups, writing multiple books, raising two daughters, building a relationship with a single wonderful woman over thirty years, becoming a patent expert, and then a professional tennis player. That alone might imply some significant depth and diversity. But it gets stranger: I am basically a trained financial expert; a lawyer; a teacher of Robotics, Tennis, Programming, Publishing, and technology in general; a very good skater and skier; a highly capable marksman with both bullets and arrows; a lover of philosophy and government; and a driver of many “classes” of vehicles (Motorcycle, Car, and Bus/MH).

All of the above is due to (a) a love of learning, (b) an ability to focus and listen, and © progressing through failure. By this point, I have become an “Execution Expert” or what I also call “A Delivery Boy”: you let me know what you want… and I will deliver that to you. As long as it doesn’t violate my morals, I can basically do almost anything. I might need others to help out, but I will find them as needed.
