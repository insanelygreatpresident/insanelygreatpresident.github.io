Author Biography

Mark Lewis Fussell was born a Quaker in Philadelphia, and was educated in Hillside elementary, Westtown School, Shipley School, and Bryn Mawr college before heading to Caltech in Pasadena.

His career has spanned Silicon Valley, Los Angeles, and India, building enterprise software, consumer software, and helping Apple win patent trials against Samsung and others. Somewhat notably, Mark and his family have survived and even thrived through ten failed startups. Mark’s first book: “The Slums of Palo Alto” (http://SlumsOfPaloAlto.com/) described valuing family, education, community, equality, kindness, and excellence so highly that progress was inevitable in spite of protracted series of failures. SOPA is available in English now, had a good (sold-out) Christmas period sale, and is being translated into Chinese.

Mark and his family live in Palo Alto, California.
